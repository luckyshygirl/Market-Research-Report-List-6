<p><p>Point-to-Multipoint Microwave Backhaul is a communication technology that facilitates data transmission from a single source to multiple endpoints using microwave frequencies. By leveraging microwave frequencies, operators can ensure high-speed data transfer with lower latency and reduced infrastructure costs.</p><p>The Point-to-Multipoint Microwave Backhaul Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.
