<p><p>The Commercial Acousto-Optic Tunable Filter (AOTF) market is segmented into two main types: collinear and noncollinear AOTFs. Collinear AOTFs involve the alignment of the acoustic and optical beams along the same axis, enabling efficient wavelength selection and high resolution. In contrast, noncollinear AOTFs have the optical and acoustic waves positioned at an angle, providing broader bandwidth and improved performance in certain applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including spectroscopy, telecommunications, and imaging technologies.</p></p>
