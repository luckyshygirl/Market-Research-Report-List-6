<p><p>The disposable glove market is categorized into four main types: latex, vinyl, nitrile, and others. Latex gloves, made from natural rubber, offer excellent elasticity and comfort but may cause allergic reactions in some users. Vinyl gloves, composed of synthetic materials, are cost-effective and suitable for low-risk tasks. Nitrile gloves, made from synthetic rubber, provide high puncture resistance and are ideal for sensitive environments. The "other" category includes specialty gloves designed for specific applications, such as chemical resistance or high-temperature protection.</p></p>
