<p><ul><li>Crude Form</li><li>Expanded Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial perlite market is primarily segmented into two types: crude form and expanded form. Crude perlite is mined directly and typically used in industries such as construction and agriculture, providing insulation and promoting soil aeration. Expanded perlite, on the other hand, undergoes heating to transform it into a lightweight, porous material, making it ideal for applications in horticulture, construction, and as a filtration aid. Both forms cater to distinct industrial needs but share a common origin.</p></p>
