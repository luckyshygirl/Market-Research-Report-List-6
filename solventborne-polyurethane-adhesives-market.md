<p><p>The Solventborne Polyurethane Adhesives market is primarily divided into two types: One-Component and Two-Component adhesives. One-Component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to moisture or heat, offering convenience and efficient application. In contrast, Two-Component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed before use, providing stronger bonds and greater versatility for demanding applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive, construction, and woodworking, due to their excellent adhesion properties and durability.</p></p>
