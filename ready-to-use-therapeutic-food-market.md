<p><p>The Ready-to-use Therapeutic Food market encompasses three main types: solid, semi-solid paste, and drinkable therapeutic foods. Solid therapeutic foods are typically energy-dense bars or biscuits designed for easy consumption and transport. Semi-solid paste formulations provide a nutrient-rich, easily digestible option, often used for malnourished patients or children. Drinkable therapeutic foods offer a convenient liquid form, allowing for rapid absorption and hydration, appealing to various age groups and dietary needs, especially in clinical settings for those with difficulty swallowing.</p></p>
