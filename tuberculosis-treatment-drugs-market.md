<p><p>The Tuberculosis Treatment Drugs market can be divided into two main types: Active TB and Latent TB. Active TB refers to cases where the bacteria causing tuberculosis are actively multiplying in the body, resulting in symptoms such as coughing, fever, and weight loss. Treatment for Active TB typically involves a combination of antibiotics taken over a period of several months. Latent TB, on the other hand, refers to cases where the bacteria are present in the body but are not actively multiplying. Treatment for Latent TB usually involves a shorter course of antibiotics to prevent the disease from becoming active in the future.</p></p>
