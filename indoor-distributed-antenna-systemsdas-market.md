<p><strong>The Indoor Distributed Antenna Systems(DAS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active DAS</li><li>Passive DAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indoor Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) enhance cellular coverage in buildings by distributing signals from a central source. Active DAS relies on powered components like repeaters and digital signal processors, providing greater flexibility and capacity for high-density environments. Conversely, Passive DAS uses simpler, unpowered components like coaxial cables and antennas, making it cost-effective for smaller installations but less capable in high traffic areas. Both types address coverage gaps, improving user experience in various indoor settings.</p></p>
