<p><p>The knee reconstruction devices market includes primary implants and revision implants. Primary implants are further categorized into cemented and cementless types. Cemented implants utilize bone cement to secure the device, providing immediate stability, while cementless implants rely on biological fixation, allowing for natural bone integration over time. Revision implants are used to address complications from previous surgeries or wear over time, ensuring proper functionality and alignment of the knee joint in patients needing corrective procedures.</p></p>
