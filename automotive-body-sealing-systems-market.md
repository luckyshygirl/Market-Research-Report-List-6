<p><strong>The Automotive Body Sealing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPDM Sealing System</li><li>PVC Sealing System</li><li>TPE Sealing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive body sealing systems market includes three primary types: EPDM sealing systems, PVC sealing systems, and TPE sealing systems. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is favored for its durability and resistance to weathering, making it ideal for exterior applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) sealing systems offer cost-effectiveness and are commonly used for interior seals. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) sealing systems combine the advantages of rubber and plastic, providing flexibility and ease of processing, suitable for diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
