<p><p>Smart Air Purifiers are available in various types including HEPA, Electrostatic Precipitators, Ionizers, and Ozone Generators. HEPA filters are effective at capturing tiny particles like dust and pollen. Electrostatic Precipitators use an electric charge to attract and trap particles. Ionizers release negatively charged ions that attach to airborne particles, causing them to fall to the ground. Ozone Generators produce ozone to neutralize odors and kill bacteria. Each type offers unique benefits for improving indoor air quality in smart air purifiers.</p></p>
