<p><p>The Internet of Things (IoT) in the automotive market can be categorized into three types: Embedded, Tethered, and Integrated. Embedded IoT systems are built into the vehicle itself, providing features like navigation and entertainment. Tethered systems rely on a smartphone or other external device for connectivity. Integrated systems combine both embedded and tethered features, offering a seamless and comprehensive experience. Each type of IoT system offers unique benefits and functionalities to enhance the overall driving experience and connectivity within the automotive industry.</p></p>
