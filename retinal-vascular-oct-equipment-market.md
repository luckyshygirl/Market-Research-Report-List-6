<p><p>The retinal vascular OCT equipment market comprises several types, primarily including Spectral Domain OCT (SD-OCT) and Swept-Source OCT. Swept-Source OCT, on the other hand, utilizes longer wavelengths, enabling deeper tissue penetration and improved visualization of retinal structures. Each type enhances the assessment and management of retinal conditions.</p></p>
