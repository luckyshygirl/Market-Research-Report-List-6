<p><strong>The Vinyl Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Extrusion</li><li>Indirect Extrusion</li><li>Hydrostatic Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vinyl extrusion equipment market is categorized into three primary types: direct extrusion, indirect extrusion, and hydrostatic extrusion. </p><p>Direct extrusion involves forcing heated vinyl material through a die to create continuous profiles. Indirect extrusion, on the other hand, uses a hollow die where the material is pushed through, allowing for more complex shapes. Hydrostatic extrusion utilizes high-pressure fluids to shape vinyl, offering reduced friction and improved surface quality. Each method serves specific applications in producing vinyl products efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
