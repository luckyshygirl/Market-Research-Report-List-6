<p><strong>The Clutch Cylinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clutch Master Cylinder(CMC)</li><li>Clutch Release Cylinder(CRC)</li><li>Clutch Slave Cylinder (CSC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clutch cylinder market comprises three main types: Clutch Master Cylinder (CMC), Clutch Release Cylinder (CRC), and Clutch Slave Cylinder (CSC). The CMC generates hydraulic pressure to engage or disengage the clutch, while the CRC facilitates the release of the clutch by transmitting this pressure. The CSC, positioned near the transmission, acts as an actuator that connects the clutch pedal's movement to the disengagement of the clutch assembly. Together, these components ensure efficient power transfer in manual transmission systems.</p></p>
