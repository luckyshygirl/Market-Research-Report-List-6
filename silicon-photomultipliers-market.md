<p><p>Silicon Photomultipliers (SiPMs) are advanced solid-state light detectors that offer high sensitivity and fast response times, making them suitable for various applications, including medical imaging, particle physics, and industrial monitoring. <p><p>The Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM) market is categorized into two main types: Analog SiPMs and Digital SiPMs. Analog SiPMs provide a continuous output signal proportional to the number of absorbed photons, making them suitable for applications requiring precise light measurement. In contrast, Digital SiPMs produce discrete digital outputs that enhance noise immunity and allow for complex signal processing. Both types are widely utilized in medical imaging, high-energy physics, and various industrial applications, offering advantages in sensitivity and operational efficiency.</p></p>
