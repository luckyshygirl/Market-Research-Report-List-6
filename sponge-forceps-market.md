<p><p>The sponge forceps market is categorized into two main types: toothed and toothless sponge forceps. Toothed sponge forceps feature serrated jaws that provide a secure grip on sponges and tissues, making them ideal for procedures that require enhanced manipulation and control. In contrast, toothless sponge forceps possess smooth jaws, allowing for a gentle hold and minimizing the risk of tissue damage. Both types are essential in surgical settings, catering to different surgical needs and preferences among medical professionals.</p></p>
