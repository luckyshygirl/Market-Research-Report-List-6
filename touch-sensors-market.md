<p><p>Touch sensors are an essential component in devices like smartphones, tablets, and laptops. Capacitive touch sensors are more sensitive and provide a smoother touch experience by detecting changes in electrical fields. Resistive touch sensors are commonly found in industrial applications, while capacitive touch sensors are predominantly used in consumer electronics.</p></p>
