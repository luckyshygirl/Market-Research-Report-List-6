<p><ul><li>RF Power Amplifiers (PAs)</li><li>RF Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF power amplifiers (PAs) are used to increase the power of RF signals before transmitting them through an antenna, making them suitable for applications such as cellular base stations and wireless communication systems. On the other hand, RF low noise amplifiers (LNAs) are designed to amplify weak signals while introducing minimal noise, making them ideal for sensitive receivers in applications like radar systems and satellite communication. Both types of RF amplifier chips play crucial roles in enabling efficient and reliable wireless communication.</p></p>
