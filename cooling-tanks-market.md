<p><p>The cooling tanks market comprises three main types: horizontal closed tanks, silo (or vertical closed) tanks, and open tanks. Horizontal closed tanks are typically used for efficient cooling in a compact design, suitable for various industrial processes. Silo tanks offer vertical storage with a larger capacity, ideal for bulk cooling applications while minimizing floor space. Open tanks allow for easy access and maintenance, commonly utilized in wastewater treatment and agricultural applications, providing effective cooling through direct exposure to the environment.</p></p>
