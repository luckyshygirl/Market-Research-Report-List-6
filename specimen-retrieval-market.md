<p><ul><li>Detachable</li><li>Non-detachable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specimen retrieval devices can be classified into two main market types: detachable and non-detachable. Detachable devices include baskets or nets that can be opened and closed to capture the specimen before being detached for removal. On the other hand, non-detachable devices are designed to grab and hold the specimen without the need for detachment. Both types have their own sets of advantages and disadvantages, and the choice between the two will depend on the specific requirements of the procedure and the preferences of the surgeon.</p></p>
