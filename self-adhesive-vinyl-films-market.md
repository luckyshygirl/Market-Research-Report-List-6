<p><ul><li>Translucent</li><li>Transparent</li><li>Opaque</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-adhesive vinyl films are categorized into three main types based on their light transmission properties: translucent, transparent, and opaque. Translucent films allow diffused light to pass through while obscuring visibility, making them ideal for privacy applications. Transparent films offer clear visibility and are often used for window graphics or protective coverings. Opaque films block all light, providing complete privacy and are commonly used for signage or branding purposes, ensuring visibility from one side only.</p></p>
