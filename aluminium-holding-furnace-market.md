<p><p>Aluminium holding furnaces are essential equipment in the aluminium industry for maintaining molten aluminium at a consistent temperature. There are three common types of aluminium holding furnaces: Pit Type, Tilting Type, and Bale-Out Type. Pit Type furnaces are installed in the ground, Tilting Type furnaces can be tilted to pour molten aluminium, and Bale-Out Type furnaces have a door at the bottom for easy removal of solidified aluminium. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs of the aluminium processing industry.</p></p>
