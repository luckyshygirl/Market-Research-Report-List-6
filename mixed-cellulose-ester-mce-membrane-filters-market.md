<p><p>The Mixed Cellulose Ester (MCE) membrane filters market includes two primary types: MCE gridded membrane filters and MCE plain membrane filters. MCE gridded filters feature a patterned grid that aids in microbial analysis and colony counting, making them ideal for application in laboratories. In contrast, MCE plain membrane filters lack this grid and are commonly used for general filtration tasks, such as air and liquid analysis. Both types are valued for their durability, versatility, and effective particle retention.</p></p>
