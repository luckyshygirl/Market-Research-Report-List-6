<p><ul><li>Primary Fatty Amine</li><li>Secondary Fatty Amine</li><li>Tertiary Fatty Amine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fatty nitrogen compounds market is segmented into primary, secondary, and tertiary fatty amines. Primary fatty amines consist of one alkyl group attached to a nitrogen atom, making them effective surfactants and emulsifiers. Secondary fatty amines have two alkyl groups, providing greater stability and varied applications in detergents and agricultural chemicals. Tertiary fatty amines, with three alkyl groups, are versatile in applications like corrosion inhibitors and ion exchange resins, offering enhanced performance in diverse industrial sectors.</p></p>
