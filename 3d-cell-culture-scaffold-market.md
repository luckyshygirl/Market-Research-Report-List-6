<p><p>3D cell culture scaffold market types include hydrogel, fiber, and other materials. Hydrogels are three-dimensional networks of hydrophilic polymer chains that provide a soft and hydrated environment for cell growth. Fiber scaffolds consist of biodegradable materials such as collagen or synthetic polymers that mimic the structure of the extracellular matrix. Other scaffolds may include decellularized matrices, microcarriers, or spheroids. These different scaffold types offer unique advantages for studying cell behavior, tissue engineering, and drug discovery in 3D culture systems.</p></p>
