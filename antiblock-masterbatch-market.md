<p><p>The antiblock masterbatch market is primarily segmented into two categories: organic and inorganic types. Organic antiblock masterbatches utilize natural substances, such as waxes or oils, to reduce surface sticking in plastics, promoting better film processing and improved clarity. In contrast, inorganic antiblock masterbatches incorporate mineral-based materials, such as silica or talc, which enhance slip performance and provide greater durability. Both types serve to enhance the functionality and quality of plastic products, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
