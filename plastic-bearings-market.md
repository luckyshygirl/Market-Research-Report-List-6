<p><strong>The Plastic Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plastic Rolling Bearings</li><li>Plastic Sliding Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic bearings market is categorized into two main types: plastic rolling bearings and plastic sliding bearings. Plastic rolling bearings utilize rolling elements like balls or rollers to reduce friction, making them suitable for high-speed applications and environments requiring corrosion resistance. In contrast, plastic sliding bearings operate through smooth surfaces gliding against each other, offering lower friction in applications with limited space and load-bearing requirements. Both types are gaining popularity due to their lightweight, durable, and cost-effective properties in various industries.</p></p>
