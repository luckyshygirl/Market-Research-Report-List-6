<p><strong>The Freediving Respiratory Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open(Open-Circuit)the Respiratory System</li><li>Enclosed(Closed-Circuit) the Respiratory System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open Circuit Freediving Respiratory Systems involve a continuous flow of air from the system into the lungs and out into the surrounding water, allowing for easy inhalation and exhalation. This system is commonly used by recreational freedivers. Enclosed Circuit Freediving Respiratory Systems, on the other hand, recirculate the exhaled air, removing carbon dioxide and replenishing oxygen, making it ideal for deep and long dives where efficiency and conservation of oxygen are crucial. This system is often used by professional competitive freedivers.</p></p>
