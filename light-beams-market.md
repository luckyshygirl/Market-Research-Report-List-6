<p><strong>The Light Beams Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Beam</li><li>Concentric Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Light Beams market can be categorized mainly into two types: Parallel Beam and Concentric Beam. Parallel Beams maintain uniform intensity and direction over long distances, making them ideal for applications like industrial lighting and alignment tasks. In contrast, Concentric Beams focus light from a central source into a circular pattern, often used in laser applications and specialized optics. Both types serve distinct purposes in various industries, including telecommunications, manufacturing, and healthcare, enhancing efficiency and precision in their respective applications.</p></p>
