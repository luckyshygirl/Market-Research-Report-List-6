<p><ul><li>Serum Sample</li><li>Plasma Sample</li><li>Whole Blood Sample</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Myoglobin Test Kit market consists of three primary types based on sample type: serum, plasma, and whole blood. Serum sample kits analyze the liquid portion of blood after coagulation, providing insights into myoglobin levels post-injury. Plasma sample kits utilize anticoagulated blood to measure myoglobin, offering quicker results due to preserved cellular components. Whole blood sample kits assess myoglobin directly from blood, providing immediate results and ease of use, making them suitable for point-of-care testing in emergency settings.</p></p>
