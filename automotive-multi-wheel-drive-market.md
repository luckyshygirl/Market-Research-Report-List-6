<p><p>The automotive multi-wheel drive market encompasses two primary systems: Four-Wheel Drive (4WD) and All-Wheel Drive (AWD). The 4WD system is designed for off-road capabilities, offering power to all four wheels, usually engaged manually or automatically under challenging conditions. In contrast, AWD provides seamless power distribution to all wheels, enhancing traction and stability on various road surfaces without driver intervention. Both systems cater to different driving needs, with 4WD focusing on rugged terrains and AWD prioritizing on-road performance and safety.</p></p>
