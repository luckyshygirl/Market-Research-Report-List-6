<p><p>Electrostatic Precipitators are air pollution control devices used to remove particles from exhaust gases. Dry Electrostatic Precipitators use an electric field to charge particles and collect them on grounded plates, while Wet Electrostatic Precipitators use water or other liquid to wash away the charged particles. Both types have their own market within the air pollution control industry, with Dry Electrostatic Precipitators being more commonly used in industries where the removal of dry particles is required, and Wet Electrostatic Precipitators being more suited for applications with sticky or corrosive particles.</p></p>
