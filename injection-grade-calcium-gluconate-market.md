<p><p>The Injection Grade Calcium Gluconate market comprises two primary types: Static Push and Static Drops. Static Push involves administering calcium gluconate in a controlled, steady manner through an injection, ideal for maintaining stable serum calcium levels. In contrast, Static Drops refer to the infusion method, where calcium gluconate is delivered via a slow drip over time, allowing for gradual absorption. Both methods are crucial in medical settings, particularly for treating calcium deficiencies and related conditions.</p></p>
