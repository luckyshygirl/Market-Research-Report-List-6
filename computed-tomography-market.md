<p><p>The Computed Tomography (CT) market is segmented into three main types based on slice capability. Low Slice CT Scanners typically offer up to 4 slices per rotation, effective for basic imaging but limited in detail. Medium Slice CT Scanners, providing 8 to 32 slices, enhance image quality and speed, suitable for a broader range of diagnostic applications. High Slice CT Scanners, with 64 slices and above, deliver superior resolution and faster imaging, making them ideal for complex evaluations and advanced medical procedures.</p></p>
