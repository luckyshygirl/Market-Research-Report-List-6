<p><ul><li>Solid Control</li><li>Containment and Handling</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drilling waste management services can be broadly categorized into three main types: solid control, containment and handling, and others. Solid control involves separating and removing solids from drilling fluids to maintain their properties. Containment and handling includes managing and disposing of drilling waste in a safe and environmentally friendly manner. The "others" category may include services such as cuttings re-injection, thermal treatment, and bioremediation. These services play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and sustainability of drilling operations.</p></p>
