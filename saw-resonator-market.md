<p><p>SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) resonators are categorized into single-port and dual-port types based on their configuration. Single-port resonators consist of one input/output terminal, commonly used in applications like filters and oscillators due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, two-port resonators have distinct input and output terminals, allowing for more complex functionalities such as signal processing and sensing applications. Each type serves specific market needs, influencing their adoption across telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive industries.</p></p>
