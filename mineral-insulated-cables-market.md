<p><p>Mineral Insulated Cables are electrical cables constructed with a copper conductor enclosed in a magnesium oxide (MgO) insulation. Mineral Insulated Cables are commonly used in applications where safety and reliability are of utmost importance.</p><p>The Mineral Insulated Cables Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.50% during the forecast period.
