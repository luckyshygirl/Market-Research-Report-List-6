<p><p>Insulated panels market consists of various types such as EPS panels, PUR/PIR panels, and Mineral/Glass Wool panels. EPS panels are made of expanded polystyrene foam, offering high insulation properties. PUR/PIR panels contain polyurethane or polyisocyanurate foam, providing excellent thermal efficiency. Mineral/Glass Wool panels are made of mineral or glass fibers, providing fire resistance and acoustic insulation. These different types cater to varying needs in terms of thermal insulation, fire safety, and soundproofing in construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
