<p><strong>The Capnography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mainstream Capnographys</li><li>Sidestream Capnographys</li><li>Microstream Capnographys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capnography equipment market includes three main types: Mainstream Capnography, Sidestream Capnography, and Microstream Capnography. Mainstream Capnography directly measures the carbon dioxide levels in exhaled air through a sensor placed at the end of an endotracheal tube. Sidestream Capnography uses a sampling tube to collect a small sample of exhaled air for analysis. Microstream Capnography is a newer technology that requires a smaller sample size and provides faster results. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different clinical settings based on their specific features and benefits.</p></p>
