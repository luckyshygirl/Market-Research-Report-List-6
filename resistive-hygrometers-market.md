<p><strong>The Resistive Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Type</li><li>Absolute Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resistive hygrometers market consists of two main types: relative and absolute hygrometers. Relative hygrometers measure the moisture content in the air compared to the maximum amount it can hold at a given temperature, providing relative humidity readings. In contrast, absolute hygrometers quantify the actual water vapor content in the air, offering a precise measurement of moisture in grams per cubic meter. Both types are essential for various applications, including meteorology, HVAC, and industrial processes, where accurate humidity control is crucial.</p></p>
