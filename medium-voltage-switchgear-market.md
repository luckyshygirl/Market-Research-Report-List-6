<p><strong>The Medium Voltage Switchgear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Insulated Switchgears (AIS)</li><li>Gas Insulated Switchgears (GIS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium voltage switchgear market encompasses primarily two types: Air Insulated Switchgears (AIS) and Gas Insulated Switchgears (GIS). AIS utilizes air as the insulating medium, requiring larger installation spaces and often being more cost-effective for outdoor applications. In contrast, GIS utilizes gas, typically sulfur hexafluoride (SF6), allowing for compact designs suitable for space-constrained environments. GIS is generally more expensive but offers advantages in reliability and lower maintenance needs, making it ideal for urban settings where space is limited.</p></p>
