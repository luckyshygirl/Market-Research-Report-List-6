<p><p>Thin-Film Deposition Machine Market consists of three main types of equipment: PVD, CVD, and ALD. PVD Equipment, or Physical Vapor Deposition Equipment, use a physical process to deposit thin films onto a substrate. CVD Equipment, or Chemical Vapor Deposition Equipment, utilize chemical reactions to form thin films on a substrate. ALD Equipment, or Atomic Layer Deposition Equipment, allow for precise control over thin film thickness and composition by depositing materials one atomic layer at a time. These different types cater to various industries and applications.</p></p>
