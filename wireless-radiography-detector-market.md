<p><strong>The Wireless Radiography Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Si(Amorphous Silicon)</li><li>Flexible Cassette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless radiography detector market is primarily segmented into two types: Amorphous Silicon (A-Si) detectors and flexible cassettes. A-Si detectors utilize a thin layer of amorphous silicon to convert X-rays into electrical signals, offering high image quality and sensitivity. Flexible cassettes, on the other hand, provide a lightweight and portable option, facilitating ease of use in various settings. Both types enhance workflow efficiency and patient comfort, contributing to the growing demand for digital radiography solutions in healthcare.</p></p>
