<p><p>Pneumology electrosurgical units (ESUs) are specialized devices used for surgical procedures involving the respiratory system. The market consists of three primary types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single active electrode to deliver current, suitable for cutting and coagulation; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, utilizing two electrodes to limit current flow, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed to efficiently seal blood vessels through controlled heating, ensuring hemostasis during thoracic surgeries.</p></p>
