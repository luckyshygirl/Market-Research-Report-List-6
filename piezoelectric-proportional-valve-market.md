<p><p>The piezoelectric proportional valve market includes two notable types: valves with bender elements and jet valves with multilayer actuators. Valves with bender elements utilize a bending motion to modulate fluid flow, offering precision control and rapid response. Jet valves with multilayer actuators employ advanced piezoelectric materials to facilitate quick actuation and fine tuning of flow rates. Both types enhance system efficiency in various applications, including automotive, industrial automation, and fluid control, by providing reliable and accurate flow manipulation.</p></p>
