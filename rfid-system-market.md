<p><strong>The RFID System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID systems are divided into two main types: Passive RFID systems and Active RFID systems. Passive RFID systems rely on external RFID readers to power the tags and collect data. This makes them more cost-effective and suitable for tracking items in close proximity. Active RFID systems, on the other hand, have tags with built-in batteries that actively transmit signals to RFID readers, allowing for longer read ranges and real-time tracking. This makes them ideal for tracking high-value assets or monitoring large areas.</p></p>
