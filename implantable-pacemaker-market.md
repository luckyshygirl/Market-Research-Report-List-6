<p><strong>The Implantable Pacemaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pacemaker</li><li>ICD</li><li>BI-V ICD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable pacemaker market includes three main types: traditional pacemakers, implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs), and biventricular ICDs (bi-V ICDs). Traditional pacemakers regulate heart rhythm for patients with bradycardia. ICDs not only pace the heart but also deliver shocks to correct life-threatening arrhythmias. Bi-V ICDs are specialized devices that provide synchronized pacing to both ventricles, improving cardiac efficiency in patients with heart failure. These devices enhance patient quality of life by managing various cardiac conditions effectively.</p></p>
