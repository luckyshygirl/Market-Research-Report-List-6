<p><ul><li>Direct Inoculation</li><li>Membrane Filtration</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterility testing market includes several key types: Direct Inoculation, Membrane Filtration, and Others. Direct Inoculation involves introducing the sample directly into a growth medium, promoting microbial growth if present. Membrane Filtration uses a filter to capture microorganisms, allowing the growth medium to test filtered samples for sterility. Other methods encompass various techniques, including rapid tests and automated systems, catering to diverse industry needs. Together, these methods ensure the safety and efficacy of pharmaceuticals and medical devices.</p></p>
