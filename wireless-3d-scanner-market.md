<p><p>The wireless 3D scanner market can be categorized into two main types: laser scanners and structured light scanners. Laser scanners utilize laser technology to capture precise 3D measurements of objects, making them suitable for applications that require high levels of accuracy. On the other hand, structured light scanners use light patterns to capture detailed 3D images of objects, making them ideal for applications that require speed and versatility. Both types of scanners are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and entertainment for a variety of purposes.</p></p>
