<p><strong>The Paint Spray Booth Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross Flow Paint</li><li>Down Draft Paint</li><li>Side Down Draft Paint</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint spray booth market is categorized into several types based on airflow and design. Cross Flow Paint Booths allow air to flow horizontally across the booth, providing effective ventilation for small jobs. Down Draft Paint Booths draw air from above and exhaust it through the floor, ensuring optimal fume extraction and surface finish. Side Down Draft Paint Booths combine features of both, drawing air from the sides and exhausting downward. Other variants may include mobile booths or custom designs, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
