<p><strong>The Neurovascular Embolectomy Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aspiration Systems</li><li>Stent Retreival Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neurovascular embolectomy device market comprises two main types: aspiration systems and stent retrieval systems. Aspiration systems utilize suction techniques to remove blood clots from cerebral vessels, enhancing blood flow and reducing ischemic damage. Stent retrieval systems involve deploying stents that physically capture and retrieve the clot, often navigating complex vascular anatomy. Both methods aim to improve outcomes in patients with acute ischemic stroke by restoring cerebral circulation, thereby minimizing potential neurological deficits and complications.</p></p>
