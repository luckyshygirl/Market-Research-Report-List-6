<p><ul><li>Switch Type</li><li>Pressure Limiting Type</li><li>Flow Or Choke Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surge suppressor unit market encompasses various types designed to manage pressure surges in systems. The switch type activates to divert or release excessive pressure, ensuring system integrity. The pressure limiting type maintains safe operational levels by automatically regulating pressure fluctuations. Flow or choke type restricts the flow during surges, protecting downstream equipment. Each type plays a crucial role in preventing damage and enhancing the reliability of industrial processes in various applications, including oil and gas, water management, and manufacturing.</p></p>
