<p><strong>The Polyester Forming Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>4-Shed Single Layer</li><li>5-Shed Single Layer</li><li>8-Shed Single Layer</li><li>8-Shed Double Layer</li><li>16-Shed Double and A Half Layer</li><li>SSB Forming Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyester forming fabric market consists of various types tailored for different industrial applications. 4-shed single layer fabrics offer basic support for lightweight paper production. 5-shed single layer designs enhance drainage and strength. 8-shed single layer fabrics provide better dimensional stability for higher-quality paper. In contrast, 8-shed double layer fabrics improve tensile strength and reduce wear. The 16-shed double and a half layer fabrics are optimal for high-speed machines, while SSB forming fabrics are designed for specialized applications requiring superior performance and durability.</p></p>
