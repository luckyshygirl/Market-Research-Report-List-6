<p><p>Adhesives and sealants for insulated glass include various types, each serving unique purposes. Silicone is flexible and suitable for high movement applications. Acrylic adhesives deliver quick curing and strong bonding. Hot-melt types offer easy application and rapid adhesion. PVC is used for its chemical resistance, whereas butyl provides strong sealing properties. Epoxy is versatile with exceptional adhesion and mechanical strength.</p></p>
