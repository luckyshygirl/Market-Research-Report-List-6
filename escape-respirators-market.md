<p><ul><li>Air Purifying Escape Respirator</li><li>Self Contained Escape Respirator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The escape respirator market is primarily categorized into two types: Air Purifying Escape Respirators (APERs) and Self-Contained Escape Respirators (SCERs). APERs utilize filtration systems to remove contaminants from the air, making them suitable for environments with specific hazards. Conversely, SCERs are self-contained units that provide breathable air from a tank, protecting users in more hazardous conditions or oxygen-deficient environments. Both types are essential for ensuring safety during emergencies where immediate evacuation is necessary.</p></p>
