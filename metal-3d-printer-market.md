<p><strong>The Metal 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal 3D Printer market features various technologies, primarily Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM uses high-powered lasers to fuse metal powder layer by layer, delivering high precision and versatility in materials. EBM, on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powders, enabling faster production rates and effective heat management. Both technologies cater to industries like aerospace and automotive, providing solutions for complex geometries and lightweight components.</p></p>
