<p><p>Nitrile rubber market primarily consists of two significant types: Hydrogenated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (HNBR) and Carboxylated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (XNBR). HNBR offers enhanced heat, oil, and chemical resistance, making it suitable for automotive seals and high-performance applications. XNBR, on the other hand, features improved abrasion resistance and tear strength, commonly used in tires and industrial applications. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and oil and gas, driving their demand in various market segments.</p></p>
