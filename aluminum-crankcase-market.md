<p><ul><li>Upper Crankcase</li><li>Lower Crankcase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum crankcase market is divided into two main types: upper crankcase and lower crankcase. Upper crankcases are typically located above the crankshaft and house components such as the cylinder head and valves. They are designed to provide protection and support for these critical engine elements. On the other hand, lower crankcases are situated below the crankshaft and contain components like the pistons and connecting rods. Both upper and lower crankcases play essential roles in maintaining the functionality and efficiency of the engine.</p></p>
