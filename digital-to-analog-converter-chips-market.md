<p><p>The Digital to Analog Converter (DAC) chips market encompasses various types, each serving specific applications. Flash DACs offer high-speed conversion with minimal latency, suitable for telecommunications. Folding DACs efficiently handle high-resolution signals while reducing power consumption. Successive Approximation Register (SAR) DACs balance speed and accuracy, commonly used in low-power applications. Pipelined DACs provide high throughput, ideal for video and audio applications. Other types may include sigma-delta and R-2R DACs, catering to specialized needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
