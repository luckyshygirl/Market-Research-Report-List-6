<p><p>Image and video capture cards can be divided into two main types based on how they connect to a computer: PCI/PCIe Capture Cards and USB Capture Cards. PCI/PCIe Capture Cards are installed directly into a computer's motherboard, offering a more stable and higher performance connection. On the other hand, USB Capture Cards are external devices that can be easily connected to a computer via a USB port, providing a more convenient and portable option for capturing images and videos. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
