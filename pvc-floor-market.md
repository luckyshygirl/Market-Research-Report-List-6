<p><p>The PVC floor market comprises various types tailored to diverse needs. Homogeneous PVC flooring features a consistent material and thickness throughout, ensuring durability and ease of maintenance. Heterogeneous PVC flooring consists of multiple layers for added design options and cushioning. Vinyl tiles (VT) provide versatility and are easy to install, while luxury vinyl tiles (LVT) offer high-end aesthetics with enhanced durability and water resistance. Each type caters to specific applications in residential and commercial settings, emphasizing functionality and style.</p></p>
