<p><ul><li>Single-Spark</li><li>Multi-Spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ignition coil market can be segmented into single-spark and multi-spark types. Single-spark ignition coils produce one spark per engine cycle, commonly used in standard gasoline engines for efficient combustion. In contrast, multi-spark ignition coils generate multiple sparks during a single cycle, enhancing the combustion process and improving engine performance, particularly in high-performance and racing applications. Each type serves specific vehicle requirements, influencing factors such as power output, fuel efficiency, and emission levels.</p></p>
