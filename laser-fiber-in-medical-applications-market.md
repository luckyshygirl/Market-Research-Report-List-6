<p><p>The medical applications market for laser fibers is divided into two main types: disposable and reusable laser fibers. Disposable laser fibers are designed for single-use purposes, enhancing patient safety and reducing the risk of cross-contamination. These are commonly used in outpatient procedures where efficiency is crucial. On the other hand, reusable laser fibers are engineered for multiple uses, providing cost-effectiveness over time and sustainability in healthcare settings. These fibers are typically sterilized and maintained for ongoing applications in various medical procedures.</p></p>
