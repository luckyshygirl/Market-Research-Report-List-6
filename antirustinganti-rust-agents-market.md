<p><p>The antirusting/anti-rust agents market comprises three main types. Water-soluble antirust agents are based on water and are primarily used for temporary protection during manufacturing processes. Oil-soluble antirust agents provide long-term protection, suitable for applications requiring excellent lubrication and rust prevention in metalworking fluids. Emulsion-type antirust agents blend water and oil, offering versatility and enhanced protection for both ferrous and non-ferrous metals. These types cater to various industrial needs, optimizing performance and corrosion resistance in diverse environments.</p></p>
