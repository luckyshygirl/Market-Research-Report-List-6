<p><p>The holographic grating market is categorized primarily into two types: plane type holographic gratings and concave type holographic gratings. Plane type holographic gratings are flat surfaces that diffract light for various applications, including spectroscopy and telecommunications, providing high efficiency and resolution. In contrast, concave type holographic gratings have a curved surface, allowing them to focus light and are often utilized in optical systems and devices like spectrometers and lasers. Both types serve distinct roles in enhancing optical performance across various industries.</p></p>
