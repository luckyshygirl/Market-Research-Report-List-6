<p><p>Embedded Non-volatile Memory (eNVM) encompasses various memory types used in electronic devices to retain data without power. Key market types include eFlash, which offers high density and performance; eE2PROM, known for its endurance and flexibility; eOTP/eMTP, which allows for one-time programmable or multiple-time programming; eFRAM, combining high speed with low power consumption; and eMRAM, providing fast access with extensive cycling endurance. Other emerging technologies continue to diversify the eNVM landscape, catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
