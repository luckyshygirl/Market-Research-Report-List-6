<p><p>The UV PVD coatings market comprises three primary types: UV Base-coat, UV Mid-coat, and UV Top-coat. The UV Base-coat serves as the foundation, enhancing adhesion and providing corrosion resistance. The UV Mid-coat adds depth and color while improving durability and scratch resistance. Finally, the UV Top-coat offers a protective layer that enhances gloss and provides additional resistance to wear and environmental factors. Together, these coatings cater to industries seeking superior finish and longevity for various substrates.</p></p>
