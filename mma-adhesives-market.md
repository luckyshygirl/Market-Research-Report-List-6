<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Adhesives</li><li>Thermoplasticity Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MMA adhesives market comprises two primary types: thermosetting adhesives and thermoplastic adhesives. Thermosetting adhesives cure permanently upon heating or chemical reaction, providing strong bonding and excellent heat resistance, making them ideal for applications in automotive and aerospace industries. In contrast, thermoplastic adhesives can be reheated and reshaped, offering flexibility and easy application, which suits packaging and construction sectors. Both types play vital roles in various industrial applications, ensuring durability and performance in bonding operations.</p></p>
