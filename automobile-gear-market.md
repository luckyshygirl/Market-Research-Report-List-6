Skew Shaft Gears are used when the shafts are not parallel and intersect at an angle. Intersecting Shaft Gears are designed for shafts that intersect at a 90-degree angle. Parallel Shaft Gears are utilized when the shafts are parallel to each other. These different gear types cater to specific needs and requirements in the automotive industry, providing solutions for various transmission systems.</p></p>
