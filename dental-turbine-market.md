<p><p>The dental turbine market is categorized into two main types based on exhaust design: Inside Exhaust and Outside Exhaust systems. Inside Exhaust turbines expel air and debris internally, creating a compact design ideal for precise procedures and minimizing external contamination. In contrast, Outside Exhaust turbines discharge air externally, providing improved visibility and reducing heat buildup during use. Both designs cater to specific dental needs, balancing efficiency and comfort for practitioners while enhancing patient safety and procedure effectiveness.</p></p>
