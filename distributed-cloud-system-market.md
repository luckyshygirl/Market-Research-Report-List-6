<p><p>A Distributed Cloud System is a model in which cloud computing resources are distributed across multiple physical locations, but the operation, governance, and evolution of the environment are the responsibility of a central, coordinating authority. This can lead to improved performance, reduced latency, and enhanced security.</p><p>The Distributed Cloud System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period.
