<p><p>The Micromobility Telematics System Market comprises three main types: embedded, tethered, and integrated systems. Embedded systems are built directly into vehicles, allowing for seamless data collection and monitoring. Tethered systems connect via external devices, often relying on smartphones for telematics functions. Integrated systems combine both approaches, offering comprehensive solutions by merging hardware and software capabilities. Each type enhances user experience and operational efficiency in the micromobility sector by providing real-time tracking, performance analytics, and connectivity.</p></p>
