<p><p>The Maleic Anhydride Grafted Polypropylene (MAPP) market is segmented into two main types: Homopolymer and Copolymer. Homopolymer MAPP is derived from polypropylene homopolymer and offers high strength and stiffness, making it suitable for applications like automotive components. In contrast, Copolymer MAPP, produced from polypropylene copolymer, exhibits improved flexibility and impact resistance, which is advantageous for packaging and consumer goods. Each type serves distinct applications, driven by their unique properties and performance characteristics, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
