<p><ul><li>Acidic Material</li><li>Alkaline Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer thinning fluid market consists of two primary types: acidic and alkaline materials. Acidic materials typically include hydrofluoric acid-based solutions that effectively etch and thin silicon wafers, enhancing surface quality and performance. Conversely, alkaline materials, such as sodium hydroxide solutions, offer a gentler approach, promoting controlled etching while minimizing damage to the wafer substrate. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, as they optimize the wafer thinning process, crucial for advanced microelectronics applications.</p></p>
