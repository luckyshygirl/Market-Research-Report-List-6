<p><p>The STATCOM UPS market can be classified into two main types based on the type of power semiconductors used: Gate Turn-Off Thyristors (GTO) STATCOM and Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors (IGBT) STATCOM. GTO STATCOMs are known for their high power handling capabilities and faster response times, making them suitable for demanding applications. On the other hand, IGBT STATCOMs are preferred for their efficiency and compact size, making them ideal for smaller scale and distributed power systems. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
