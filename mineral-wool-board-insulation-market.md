<p><p>Mineral wool board insulation encompasses three primary types: slag wool, glass wool, and rock wool. Slag wool, derived from steel mill byproducts, offers excellent thermal and acoustic insulation. Glass wool, made from recycled glass fibers, is lightweight and provides superior thermal resistance. Rock wool, originating from volcanic rock, delivers high fire resistance and soundproofing properties. These insulation materials are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications, enhancing energy efficiency and environmental sustainability in building projects.</p></p>
