<p><p>The push-pull dynamometer market comprises two primary types: electronic medical dynamometers and mechanical medical dynamometers. Electronic medical dynamometers utilize sensors and digital displays to provide precise measurements of force, benefiting rehabilitation and diagnostic processes. In contrast, mechanical medical dynamometers rely on spring-based systems and analog readouts, offering a more straightforward, often cost-effective solution for measuring muscle strength and endurance. Both types cater to healthcare professionals' needs in assessing patient capabilities and supporting effective treatment plans.</p></p>
