<p><strong>The Electron Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs)</li><li>Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron microscope market primarily consists of two main types: Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs) and Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs). SEMs provide detailed surface imaging by scanning a sample with a focused beam of electrons, ideal for material science and biology applications. In contrast, TEMs transmit electrons through ultra-thin samples, offering high-resolution internal structure analysis, making them essential for advanced research in nanotechnology and semiconductor development. Both types serve critical roles in scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
