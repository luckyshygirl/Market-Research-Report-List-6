<p><strong>The Hypercholesterolemia Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Statins</li><li>Non-Statins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hypercholesterolemia drugs market comprises two primary categories: statins and non-statins. Statins, such as atorvastatin and simvastatin, are widely prescribed to lower LDL cholesterol by inhibiting the enzyme HMG-CoA reductase. Non-statin therapies include a range of drugs like ezetimibe, which reduces cholesterol absorption, and PCSK9 inhibitors that enhance LDL clearance from the bloodstream. Both types play crucial roles in managing cholesterol levels, supporting cardiovascular health, and addressing various patient needs and treatment responses.</p></p>
