<p><strong>The Automotive Radiators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Down-Flow</li><li>Cross-Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive radiator market is primarily divided into two types: down-flow and cross-flow radiators. Down-flow radiators direct coolant vertically from the top to the bottom, offering efficient cooling for various vehicle designs, especially older models. In contrast, cross-flow radiators allow coolant to flow horizontally, typically featuring a more compact design suitable for modern vehicles. Each type serves distinct cooling needs and optimizes engine performance, with manufacturers selecting designs based on vehicle specifications and thermal management requirements.</p></p>
