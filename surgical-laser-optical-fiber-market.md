<p><p>The surgical laser optical fiber market can be categorized into disposable and reusable types. Disposable optical fibers are designed for single-use, ensuring maximum hygiene and minimizing infection risk, making them ideal for procedures requiring high levels of sterility. Reusable optical fibers are built for multiple uses, often more economical over time, but necessitate rigorous cleaning and sterilization processes after each use to maintain efficacy and patient safety. Both types cater to specific surgical requirements and preferences in medical settings.</p></p>
