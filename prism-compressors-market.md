<p><ul><li>Prismatic Pulse Compressors</li><li>Multiple-Prism Pulse Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prism compressors are essential components in laser technology, used to compress laser pulses for various applications. The prismatic pulse compressor utilizes a single prism to manipulate and shorten pulse widths, which enhances the peak power of laser beams. In contrast, multiple-prism pulse compressors employ a series of prisms, allowing for greater control and efficiency in pulse compression, leading to even shorter pulse durations and higher energy outputs. Both types cater to various fields such as telecommunications, medical, and industrial applications.</p></p>
