<p><strong>The Airsoft Guns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spring-powered</li><li>Battery-powered</li><li>Gas-powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airsoft guns market encompasses three main types: spring-powered, battery-powered, and gas-powered. Spring-powered guns operate using a mechanical spring, requiring manual cocking for each shot, making them simple and reliable. Battery-powered guns, or AEGs, use electric motors and rechargeable batteries for automatic firing, offering rapid shooting and ease of use. Gas-powered guns utilize compressed gas for propulsion, providing realistic recoil and higher power, suitable for advanced players. Each type appeals to different preferences and skill levels in the sport.</p></p>
