<p><p>Arc Fault Circuit Interrupters (AFCIs) enhance electrical safety by detecting dangerous arc faults and interrupting the circuit to prevent fires. The market features several types: Branch/Feeder AFCIs protect entire circuits, while Combination AFCIs (CAFCIs) safeguard against both series and parallel arcs. Outlet AFCIs are incorporated into specific outlets for localized protection. Additionally, other variations cater to unique applications and industry requirements, ensuring comprehensive solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial electrical safety.</p></p>
