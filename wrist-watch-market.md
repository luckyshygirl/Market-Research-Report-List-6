<p><p>The wristwatch market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical watches and electronic watches. Mechanical watches operate through intricate gear systems and rely on winding, either manually or through automatic movement. They appeal to purists who appreciate craftsmanship. In contrast, electronic watches use quartz crystals for precise timekeeping powered by batteries or solar energy, making them more accurate and low-maintenance. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, blending tradition with modern technology in the watch industry.</p></p>
