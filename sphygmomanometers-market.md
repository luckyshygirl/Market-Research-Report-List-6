<p><p>The sphygmomanometer market consists of three primary types: Mercury, Aneroid, and Digital sphygmomanometers. Mercury sphygmomanometers utilize mercury columns for accurate blood pressure measurement but are being phased out due to safety concerns. Aneroid sphygmomanometers use calibrated dial displays and require manual inflation, offering portability and durability. Digital sphygmomanometers provide ease of use with automatic readings and are increasingly popular for home monitoring. Each type serves unique user needs and preferences in clinical or personal settings.</p></p>
