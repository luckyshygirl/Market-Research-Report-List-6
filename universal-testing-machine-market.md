<p><strong>The Universal Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical UTM</li><li>Hydraulic UTM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Universal Testing Machine (UTM) market primarily comprises two types: electromechanical and hydraulic UTMs. Electromechanical UTMs utilize electric motors and lead screws to apply precise loads, making them suitable for a wide range of materials testing, including metals and plastics. In contrast, hydraulic UTMs employ hydraulic systems to exert high forces, ideal for testing larger specimens and applications requiring greater load capacities. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, aerospace, and material science, fulfilling various testing needs.</p></p>
