<p><p>The Zirconium Rod market encompasses three primary types: hot rolled, forged, and cold worked. Hot rolled zirconium rods are produced through heating and deforming the metal, resulting in improved ductility and strength. Forged zirconium rods are created by applying pressure to shape the metal, enhancing its mechanical properties and performance. Cold working involves deforming zirconium at room temperature, which increases hardness and allows for precise dimensions. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, catering to various performance requirements.</p></p>
