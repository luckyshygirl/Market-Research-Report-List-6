<p><p>Electron tubes, also known as vacuum tubes, are electronic devices that control electric currents in a high vacuum between electrodes. Despite being largely overshadowed by semiconductor technology, electron tubes continue to find applications in niche markets such as audio amplification, radio frequency amplification, and in certain high-power industrial equipment.</p><p>The Electron Tubes Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of -2.00% during the forecast period.
