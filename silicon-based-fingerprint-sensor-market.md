<p><strong>The Silicon Based Fingerprint Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Touch Type</li><li>Slide Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon-based fingerprint sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: touch type and slide type. Touch type sensors capture fingerprints by placing a finger directly on a scanning surface, providing high-resolution images for secure authentication. These sensors are commonly used in smartphones and access control systems. On the other hand, slide type sensors require the user to swipe their finger across a scanning area, enabling continuous and quick scanning, often used in handheld devices and biometric systems for enhanced user experiences.</p></p>
