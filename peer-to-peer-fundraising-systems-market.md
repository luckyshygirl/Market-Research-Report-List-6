<p><p>Peer-to-peer fundraising systems can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, offering greater accessibility and scalability, allowing organizations to manage campaigns from anywhere with internet access. On-premises systems, on the other hand, are installed directly on an organization's local servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. Both types serve to enhance fundraising efforts by facilitating user engagement and donation management.</p></p>
