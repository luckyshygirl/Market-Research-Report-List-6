<p><p>The continuous flow centrifuge market comprises several types designed for various laboratory and industrial applications. Benchtop centrifuges are compact and ideal for small-scale use, while floor-standing centrifuges offer larger capacity and power for higher throughput. Micro centrifuges are specialized for small sample volumes, often in molecular biology. Specialty centrifuges serve specific purposes, such as separating blood components. Ultracentrifuges operate at extremely high speeds, enabling the separation of sub-cellular components and macromolecules, crucial for advanced research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
