<p><p>The advanced CO2 sensors market primarily includes NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) sensors and chemical CO2 sensors. NDIR sensors operate by measuring the infrared light absorption of CO2 molecules, offering high accuracy and stability, making them ideal for continuous monitoring in various applications. In contrast, chemical CO2 sensors utilize chemical reactions to detect CO2 levels, often providing cost-effective solutions for specific industries. Both types serve critical roles in environmental monitoring, industrial applications, and indoor air quality management.</p></p>
