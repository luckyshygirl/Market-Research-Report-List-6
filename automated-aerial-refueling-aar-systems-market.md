<p><p>Automated Aerial Refueling (AAR) systems are essential for extending the operational range of aerial platforms. The market comprises two primary types: Probe and Drogue systems, which use a flexible hose with a drogue at the end that the aircraft's probe connects to; and Boom and Receptacle systems, utilizing a rigid pole (boom) that fuels the receiving aircraft through a receptacle. Each type offers unique advantages, with differences in compatibility, ease of use, and operational efficiency tailored to specific military and commercial applications.</p></p>
