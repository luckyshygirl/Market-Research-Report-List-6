<p><p>Elastoforming presses are categorized into two main types: electric and hydraulic. Electric elastoforming presses use electrical power to generate the force needed for forming operations, offering more precision and control. On the other hand, hydraulic elastoforming presses use hydraulic power to provide high forces for heavy-duty applications. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific application requirements. Electric presses are preferred for accuracy and control, while hydraulic presses are favored for their high force capabilities.</p></p>
