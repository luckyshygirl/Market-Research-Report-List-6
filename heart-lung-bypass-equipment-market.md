<p><p>The Heart Lung Bypass Equipment market is primarily segmented into two types: Single Roller Pump and Double Roller Pump Cardiopulmonary Bypass Equipment. Single Roller Pump systems utilize a single pump to manage blood flow during surgery, offering simplicity and ease of maintenance. In contrast, Double Roller Pump systems employ two parallel pumps for enhanced flow stability and redundancy, allowing for more precise control of blood circulation. Both types are essential for maintaining oxygenation and perfusion during cardiac procedures.</p></p>
