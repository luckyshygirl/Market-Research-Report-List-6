<p><p>The PVC flooring market comprises several types, including homogeneous, heterogeneous, vinyl tiles (VT), and luxury vinyl tiles (LVT). Homogeneous PVC floors feature a single-layered structure with consistent color throughout, ideal for high-traffic areas. Heterogeneous PVC floors consist of multiple layers, offering a variety of designs and enhanced performance. Vinyl tiles (VT) are versatile, easy to install, and available in various aesthetics. Luxury vinyl tiles (LVT) mimic natural materials, providing a premium look with superior durability and comfort, making them increasingly popular for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
