<p><p>The tire pressure gauge market includes two primary types: dial and digital gauges. Dial gauges feature a traditional analog display, using a needle and graduated scale to indicate pressure levels. They are often favored for their simplicity and durability. Digital gauges, on the other hand, use electronic sensors to provide precise pressure readings on an LCD screen. They are generally easier to read and may offer added features such as automatic shutoff and backlighting. Both types serve essential roles in vehicle maintenance and safety.</p></p>
