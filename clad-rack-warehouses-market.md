<p><p>Clad rack warehouses typically utilize two main types of racking: pallet racks and cantilever racks. Pallet racks are designed for storing palletized goods, maximizing vertical space and ensuring efficient access. They are ideal for warehouses with a high volume of boxed or palletized items. In contrast, cantilever racks are suited for long or irregularly shaped items, such as lumber or pipes, allowing easy access and visibility. Both types enhance storage efficiency, organization, and accessibility in warehouse operations.</p></p>
