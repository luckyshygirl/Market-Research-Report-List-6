<p><p>The Light Gauge Steel Framing market consists of several types based on structural design and application. Skeleton framing features a framework of steel columns and beams that supports the building's load, allowing for flexibility in interior layout. Wall-bearing framing relies on steel studs for load support and is commonly used in low-rise structures. Long span framing is designed for large, open spaces, minimizing support requirements, making it ideal for commercial and industrial applications. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific construction needs.</p></p>
