<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solventless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cabinet adhesives market is categorized into three primary types: solvent-based, water-based, and solventless adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding and quick drying, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications but may emit volatile organic compounds. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, low in odor, and safe to use, ideal for indoor applications. Solventless adhesives, which contain no solvents, are increasingly popular for their eco-friendliness and strong bonds, catering to manufacturers seeking sustainable solutions in cabinetry and furniture production.</p></p>
