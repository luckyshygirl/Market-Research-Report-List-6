<p><ul><li>Flotation</li><li>Electrical Seperation</li><li>Re-election</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphite beneficiation machine market is segmented into three main types: flotation, electrical separation, and re-election. Flotation involves using chemicals to selectively separate graphite from impurities, enhancing purity. Electrical separation relies on the difference in conductivity between graphite and other materials, efficiently isolating graphite. Re-election, or gravity separation, utilizes the density differences of particles, effectively separating high-density graphite from lighter waste. Each method targets improved graphite purity and yield for various applications across industries.</p></p>
