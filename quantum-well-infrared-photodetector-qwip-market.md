<p><p>The Quantum Well Infrared Photodetector (QWIP) market can be categorized into two main types: Light Guide Type and Photovoltaic Type. The Light Guide Type uses optical fibers or guides to direct infrared light to the detector, enhancing sensitivity and performance in specific applications. In contrast, the Photovoltaic Type generates an electrical response directly from the incoming infrared photons, providing efficient signal conversion. Both types serve critical roles in various fields, including security, environmental monitoring, and medical imaging, leveraging their unique advantages.</p></p>
