<p><p>The bio-based chemicals market includes various types, each derived from renewable resources. Starch blends are made from starch and other materials, offering biodegradable alternatives. Regenerated cellulose is produced from natural cellulose fibers, used for sustainable textiles and packaging. Polybutylene succinate (PBS) provides compostable plastics, while poly(ethylene terephthalate) (Bio-PET) is a bio-derived version of PET, often utilized in bottles. Polylactic acid (PLA) is a biodegradable polymer from corn starch, widely used in packaging and disposable products, promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
