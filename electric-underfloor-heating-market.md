<p><ul><li>Heating Cable</li><li>Electrothermal</li><li>Carbon Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric underfloor heating market comprises three main types: heating cables, electrothermal systems, and carbon crystal heaters. Heating cables involve electrical wires that generate heat and are installed beneath flooring. Electrothermal systems utilize a network of heating mats or films to distribute warmth evenly across the floor surface. Carbon crystal heaters, on the other hand, use carbon-based materials to provide efficient thermal radiation. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various installation preferences and energy efficiency requirements in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
