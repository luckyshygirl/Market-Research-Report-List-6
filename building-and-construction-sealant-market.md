Plastisol sealants are commonly used for sealing metal joints, while emulsion sealants are water-based and suitable for interior applications. Polysulfide sealants deliver excellent chemical resistance, making them suitable for industrial settings. Butyl-based sealants offer strong bonding and durability for roofing and glazing applications.</p></p>
