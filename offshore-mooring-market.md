<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Offshore Mooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA)</li><li>Vertical Load Anchors (VLA)</li><li>Suction Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore mooring market features various anchor types, including Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA), Vertical Load Anchors (VLA), and Suction Anchors. DEAs are designed to embed into the seabed by dragging, providing stable holding power for floating structures. VLAs utilize vertical loads for anchoring, suitable for deeper waters with significant lateral forces. Suction Anchors create a vacuum to embed into the seabed, offering high holding capacity and minimal environmental disruption, making them ideal for dynamic offshore applications.</p></p>
