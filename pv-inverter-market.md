<p><p>PV inverters are essential components of solar power systems that convert DC electricity generated by solar panels into usable AC electricity. There are three main types of PV inverters on the market: string inverters, central inverters, and microinverters. </p><p>String inverters are connected to multiple solar panels in a series, central inverters are used in larger installations and connected to all panels, and microinverters are smaller units attached to individual panels. Each type of inverter has its own advantages and is suitable for different sizes and designs of solar power systems.</p></p>
