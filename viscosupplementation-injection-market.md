<p><p>Viscosupplementation injection is a medical treatment primarily used for osteoarthritis, particularly in the knee joint. This procedure involves injecting hyaluronic acid (HA) into the joint space to restore viscosity and lubricating properties, alleviating pain and improving mobility. The treatment serves as an alternative to more invasive procedures like surgery and has gained traction due to its minimally invasive nature and relatively quick recovery time.</p><p>The Viscosupplementation Injection Market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 5.70% during the forecast period. Additionally, enhancements in injection techniques and formulations are contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Key trends within the market include the development of innovative products with improved efficacy and longer-lasting results, the incorporation of combination therapies that integrate viscosupplementation with stem cell therapy or PRP, and a shift towards patient-centric care approaches.
