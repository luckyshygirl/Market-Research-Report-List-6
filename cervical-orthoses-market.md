<p><p>Cervical orthoses market consists of two main types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to provide support while allowing some degree of movement, making them ideal for individuals recovering from neck injuries. On the other hand, static orthotics are rigid and provide full support, often used for conditions requiring immobilization of the neck. Both types aim to stabilize the cervical spine and alleviate pain, but they differ in their level of rigidity and flexibility.</p></p>
