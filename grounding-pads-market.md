<p><p>Grounding pads are categorized into monopolar and bipolar types, each serving distinct applications. Monopolar grounding pads utilize a single electrode to establish a connection with the ground, often used in simpler setups for personal or industrial use. In contrast, bipolar grounding pads feature two electrodes, allowing for a balanced electrical discharge, which can enhance effectiveness in therapeutic applications. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring safety and efficacy in various electrical and medical contexts, targeting different user needs.</p></p>
