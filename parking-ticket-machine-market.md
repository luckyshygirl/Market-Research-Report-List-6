<p><ul><li>Parking Meter (Single Space)</li><li>Parking Kiosks (Multi Space)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parking ticket machine market comprises two primary types: single-space parking meters and multi-space parking kiosks. Single-space meters are installed at individual parking spots, allowing users to pay for their specific space, typically using coins or mobile payments. In contrast, multi-space kiosks serve multiple parking spots, enabling users to pay for several spaces at once, often through touchscreen interfaces. Both systems improve parking management, streamline payment processes, and enhance user convenience while offering municipalities a means to regulate and monitor parking efficiently.</p></p>
