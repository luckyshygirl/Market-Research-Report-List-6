<p><strong>The Subcutaneous Implantable Defibrillator System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Zone</li><li>Dual Zone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Subcutaneous Implantable Defibrillator (S-ICD) system market is categorized into two main types: Single Zone and Dual Zone. The Single Zone S-ICD provides defibrillation therapy for patients at risk of sudden cardiac arrest, focusing solely on one defibrillation zone. In contrast, the Dual Zone system includes an additional zone for monitoring and treating patients with both bradycardia and tachycardia, allowing for more comprehensive management of cardiac arrhythmias. Each type addresses specific patient needs in cardiac care.</p></p>
