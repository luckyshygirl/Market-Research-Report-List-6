<p><p>The optical lens edger market comprises three main types: manual, automatic, and semi-automatic edgers. Manual optical lens edgers require operators to manually shape and finish lenses, offering greater control but demanding more skill and time. Automatic optical lens edgers streamline the process, using advanced technology to achieve precision and efficiency with minimal human intervention. Semi-automatic optical lens edgers blend both approaches, allowing operator input while automating certain steps, making them ideal for various production scales and operational needs.</p></p>
