<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Pipe Handling Systems</li><li>Electric Pipe Handling Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipe handling systems are used in drilling rigs to handle and position pipes during drilling operations. There are two main types of pipe handling systems in the market: Hydraulic and Electric. Hydraulic systems use fluid power to operate the mechanisms that handle the pipes, providing high efficiency and precision. On the other hand, Electric systems use electrical power for operation, offering a more automated and hands-free approach to pipe handling. Both systems have their own advantages and are widely used in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
