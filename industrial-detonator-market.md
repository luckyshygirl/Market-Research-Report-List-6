<p><strong>The Industrial Detonator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prompt Detonator</li><li>Delay Detonator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial detonator market primarily comprises two types: prompt detonators and delay detonators. Prompt detonators initiate explosive devices instantly upon activation, providing immediate blasting effects. In contrast, delay detonators are designed to create a timed delay between the triggering event and the explosion, allowing for sequential blasting and improved safety management in operations. Both types are essential in various sectors, including mining, construction, and demolition, facilitating efficient and controlled explosive applications tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
