<p><p>Cryotubes are specialized containers used for the storage of biological samples at low temperatures. The market is segmented into two primary types based on cap design: External Thread Screw Caps and Internal Thread Screw Caps. External Thread Screw Caps feature threads on the outside of the tube, providing a secure seal for samples, making handling easier. Internal Thread Screw Caps, conversely, have threads on the inside, allowing for a tighter closure and better sample protection against contamination. Both types cater to specific storage needs and preferences in laboratories.</p></p>
