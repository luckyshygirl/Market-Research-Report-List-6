<p><p>The femtosecond laser equipment market is typically categorized into full femtosecond and half femtosecond types. Full femtosecond lasers produce pulses shorter than 1 picosecond, offering high precision for applications in materials processing, medical procedures, and scientific research. Half femtosecond lasers, with pulse durations typically in the range of 1 to 10 picoseconds, provide a balance between speed and energy, making them suitable for various applications requiring moderate precision. Both types are essential in advancing laser technology and its diverse applications.</p></p>
