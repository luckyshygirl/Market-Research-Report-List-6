<p><p>Pressure sensitive paper is categorized into several market types based on thickness and application. HHS (High Heavy Stock) is used for demanding applications requiring durability. HS (Heavy Stock) provides a balance of thickness and flexibility, suitable for various labels. MS (Medium Stock) is versatile for light to moderate use. LW (Lightweight) caters to cost-sensitive projects. LLW (Light Lightweight), LLLW (Super Lightweight), and 4LW (Ultra Lightweight) focus on low-cost, high-volume applications, ideal for temporary labels and packaging.</p></p>
