<p><p>Stainless Steel Ball Valves come in different types based on their construction - One Piece, Two Piece, and Three Piece. One Piece Stainless Steel Ball Valves are integrally machined from a single piece of stainless steel, providing a more sturdy and leak-proof design. Two Piece Stainless Steel Ball Valves consist of two separate pieces that are bolted together, allowing for easier maintenance and repair. Three Piece Stainless Steel Ball Valves have a body made of three separate pieces, making them ideal for applications requiring frequent maintenance or cleaning.</p></p>
