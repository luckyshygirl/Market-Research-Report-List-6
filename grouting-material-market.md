<p><p>Grouting materials can be categorized into two main types: urethane based grout and epoxy based grout. Urethane based grout is known for its flexibility and resistance to cracking, making it ideal for areas with movement or vibration. On the other hand, epoxy based grouts have high strength and excellent chemical resistance, making them suitable for use in heavy-duty applications. Both types offer different advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the project at hand.</p></p>
