<p><p>The Industrial Mercury Emissions Analyser market includes two main types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzers and Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzers. Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzers utilize a cold vapor technique to analyze mercury levels in samples, providing high sensitivity and accuracy. On the other hand, Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzers use a cold atomic fluorescence technique to measure mercury emissions, offering lower detection limits and higher precision. Both types of analyzers play a crucial role in monitoring and controlling industrial mercury emissions.</p></p>
