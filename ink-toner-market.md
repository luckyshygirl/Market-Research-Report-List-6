<p><strong>The Ink & Toner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye Ink</li><li>Pigment Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ink and toner market is primarily divided into two types: dye ink and pigment ink. Dye ink is water-soluble and provides vibrant colors, making it ideal for high-quality photo printing. However, it is less resistant to fading and water damage. In contrast, pigment ink consists of solid particles suspended in liquid, offering superior durability and color fastness. This makes pigment ink suitable for archival prints and materials exposed to environmental factors, catering to diverse printing needs in various industries.</p></p>
