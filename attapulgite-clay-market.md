<p><p>Attapulgite clay, known for its unique properties, is categorized primarily into colloidal and sorptive types in the market. Colloidal attapulgite is valued for its ability to create stable suspensions in liquids, making it ideal for use in paints, drilling fluids, and pharmaceuticals. Sorptive attapulgite, on the other hand, excels in adsorption, drawing in and holding onto various substances, which is beneficial in applications like environmental remediation, catalysis, and as an absorbent in pet litter and industrial spills.</p></p>
