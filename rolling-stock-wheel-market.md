<p><strong>The Rolling Stock Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rolling stock wheel market comprises two primary types: casting and forged wheels. Casting involves pouring molten metal into molds to create wheels, allowing for complex shapes and efficient mass production. This method is often cost-effective for large-scale manufacturing. In contrast, forged wheels are produced by shaping metal under high pressure, resulting in superior strength and durability. This process is ideal for high-performance applications. Both types serve specific needs in the railway industry, balancing cost and performance effectively.</p></p>
