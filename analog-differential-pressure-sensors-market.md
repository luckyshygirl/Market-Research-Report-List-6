<p><p>Analog differential pressure sensors measure pressure differences between two points, commonly used in industrial applications. The silicon-based market utilizes microelectromechanical systems (MEMS) technology, offering high accuracy, sensitivity, and miniaturization, making them suitable for compact devices. In contrast, foil-based sensors employ metallic strain gauges to detect pressure changes, providing durability and robustness in extreme environments. Both types cater to varying industry needs, with silicon-based sensors favored for precision applications and foil-based sensors for rugged conditions.</p></p>
