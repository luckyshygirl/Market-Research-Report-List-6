<p><ul><li>Slip</li><li>Antistatic</li><li>Anti-fog</li><li>Anti-block</li><li>Antimicrobial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaging coating additives market encompasses various types designed to enhance performance and functionality. Slip additives improve the surface smoothness, enabling easy movement and reduced friction between surfaces. Antistatic additives minimize static electricity buildup, preventing dust attraction and improving handling. Anti-fog additives enhance clarity by preventing moisture condensation on surfaces. Anti-block additives reduce adhesion between layers in stacked films. Antimicrobial additives inhibit the growth of bacteria and fungi, making packaging safer and extending shelf life, particularly in food applications.</p></p>
