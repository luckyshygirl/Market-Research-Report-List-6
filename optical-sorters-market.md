<p><p>Optical sorters are advanced machines used in various industries for sorting materials based on color, shape, and size. There are two main types of optical sorters: chute-type color sorter machine and belt-type color sorter machine. Chute-type sorters use gravity to separate materials into different chutes based on color, while belt-type sorters use a conveyor belt to move materials under cameras that detect and sort by color. Both types of machines are widely used for efficient and accurate sorting processes in industries such as food processing, recycling, and mining.</p></p>
