<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) charger market is primarily divided into two types: on-board chargers and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are integrated within the EV, converting AC electricity from charging sources to DC for the vehicle's battery. They are limited by space and power capacity. Off-board chargers, on the other hand, are external charging stations that provide higher power levels, allowing for faster charging times and greater versatility in locations such as homes, workplaces, and public areas.</p></p>
