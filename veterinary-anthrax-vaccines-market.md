<p><ul><li>Cell Free PA Vaccines</li><li>Live Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Veterinary anthrax vaccines can be categorized into two main types: cell-free PA vaccines and live vaccines. Cell-free PA vaccines are made from purified protective antigen (PA) proteins derived from the anthrax bacterium, providing immunity without the risk of infection. On the other hand, live vaccines contain weakened or killed forms of the anthrax bacterium to stimulate an immune response in the animal. Both types of vaccines are crucial in preventing and controlling anthrax outbreaks in livestock and are essential tools for veterinary health.</p></p>
