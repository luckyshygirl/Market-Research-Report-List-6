<p><p>Hotel and hospitality management software can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and SaaS-based solutions. On-premises software is installed locally on a hotel's servers, offering complete control over data and customization, but often requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, SaaS-based software is cloud-hosted, allowing for easy access from anywhere with an internet connection. This model typically operates on a subscription basis, providing scalability and reduced IT responsibilities, making it a popular choice for many hospitality businesses.</p></p>
