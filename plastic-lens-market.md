<p><p>The plastic lens market comprises two primary types: aspheric lenses and spherical lenses. Aspheric lenses feature a gradual change in curvature, allowing for improved focus and reduced distortion, making them ideal for high-performance applications like cameras and eyeglasses. In contrast, spherical lenses maintain a consistent curvature, which is simpler to manufacture and widely used in basic optical devices. Both types cater to various industries, including healthcare, consumer electronics, and photography, driving innovation and meeting specific user needs.</p></p>
