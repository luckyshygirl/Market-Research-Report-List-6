<p><p>Tyres market can be categorized into Skewed Tire and Radial Tire types based on their construction. Skewed Tires have a bias ply construction with cords that run at an angle across the body of the tire, providing strength and stability. On the other hand, Radial Tires have cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better traction, fuel efficiency, and a smoother ride. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences of consumers, providing a variety of options in the tyre industry.</p></p>
