<p><p>The Polyisocyanurate insulation market comprises two primary types: spray type and foam/board type. Spray type insulation involves applying a liquid mixture that expands and cures into a seamless layer, offering excellent thermal performance and minimal gaps. Foam/board type insulation consists of rigid boards that provide high insulation values and can be cut to fit various spaces. Both types are used widely in construction for energy efficiency, reducing heat transfer, and meeting building codes for thermal resistance.</p></p>
