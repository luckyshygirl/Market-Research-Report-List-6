<p><p>The electric vehicle traction batteries market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Open Lead Acid Battery**: Traditional, cost-effective, but heavy with limited cycle life.</p><p>2. **Pure Lead Battery**: Offers improved performance and durability over standard lead-acid, suited for high-drain applications.</p><p>3. **Gel Battery**: Sealed and maintenance-free with a stable discharge rate, ideal for deep cycling.</p><p>4. **Lithium-Ion Battery**: Lightweight, high energy density, and long cycle life, dominating the market due to efficiency and fast charging capabilities. </p><p>These batteries cater to different performance and cost requirements in electric vehicles.</p></p>
